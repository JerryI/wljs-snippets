---
title: Notes on Slide cells
desc: Instructions on how to make presentations in a notebook
---

You can also print cells, that represents slides! It uses Reveal.JS with Markdown as a main language to describe the content of a slide.
__Just mention "RevealJS" or "Reveal" in the language argument when you use cellPrint function__, that represents a slide.

The general rule is one cell - one slide, it is better to print another cell, if you want to have two slides in the notebook.
